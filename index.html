Sam Jones

Lecturer in Developmental Psychology at Bangor University

Speech, language, and communication skills are essential to human wellbeing. My research aims to improve understanding of neurological disorders affecting these skills through experimentation and computational modelling, with the goal of improving clinical assessment, intervention, and rehabilitation.
