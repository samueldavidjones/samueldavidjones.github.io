Samuel David Jones

Lecturer in Developmental Psychology at Bangor University

Speech, language, and communication skills are essential to human wellbeing. My research aims to accelerate the understanding of neurological disorders affecting these skills through experimentation and computational modelling, with the goal of improving clinical assessment, intervention, and rehabilitation.
