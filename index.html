Sam Jones

Senior Research Associate in Developmental Cognitive Psychology at Lancaster University

Speech, language, and communication skills are essential to human wellbeing. My research aims to accelerate understanding of lifespan neurological disorders affecting these skills through experimentation and computational modelling, with the goal of improving clinical assessment, intervention, and rehabilitation.
